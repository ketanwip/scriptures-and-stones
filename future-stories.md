## From the Ramayana (Valmiki)
The Descent of Ganga: Bhagiratha’s arduous penance to purify his ancestors.

The Churning of the Ocean (Samudra Manthan): The quest for Amrita and the emergence of Halahala.

The Curse of Ahalya: Redemption through the touch of Rama’s feet.

The Sacrifice of Rishyasringa: The unique yagya performed for the birth of Dasaratha's sons.

Vishwamitra’s Transformation: The journey from a King (Rajarshi) to a Brahmarshi.

The Tale of Sunahshepa: A story of sacrifice and divine intervention.

Hanuman’s Leap: The crossing of the ocean and the encounter with Mainaka and Surasa.

The Humbling of Varuna: Rama’s threat to dry the ocean with the Brahmastra.

Kumbhakarna’s Boon: The linguistic slip that led to eternal sleep.

The Coronation of Vibhishana: Dharma prevailing over kinship.

## From the Mahabharata (Vyasa)
The Tale of Shakuntala and Dushyanta: The ancestral roots of the Bharata lineage.

The Vow of Bhishma: Devavrata’s supreme sacrifice for his father’s happiness.

The Burning of the Khandava Forest: Krishna and Arjuna’s encounter with Agni and Maya Danava.

The Yaksha Prashna: Yudhishthira’s test of wisdom at the enchanted lake.

The Story of Savitri and Satyavan: Outwitting Yama through the power of Pativrata.

Nala and Damayanti: A saga of love, loss, and the influence of Kali Yuga.

The Ashvamedha of Yudhishthira: The post-war spiritual cleansing.

The Great Departure (Mahaprasthanika Parva): The Pandavas' final journey to the Himalayas.

The Slaughter of the Sauptika Parva: Ashwatthama’s midnight raid.

The Tale of King Shibi: Offering one's own flesh to protect a dove.

Kach and Devayani: The quest for the Sanjivani Vidya.

The Death of Jarasandha: The wrestling match of Bhima.

The Curse of Kindama: The origin of the restriction on King Pandu.

The Tale of Amba: The roots of Bhishma's downfall.

The Upamanyu Penance: Devotion to Shiva and the gift of the Milk Ocean.

## From the Puranas (Srimad Bhagavatam, Vishnu, Shiva, Devi, etc.)
The Varaha Avatar: Lifting the Earth from the Garbhodaka Ocean.

The Pralaya and Matsya Avatar: Saving the Vedas and Manu from the Great Flood.

The Legend of Dhruva: The five-year-old’s journey to becoming the Pole Star.

The Penance of Parvati: The rigorous tapas to win Lord Shiva.

The Slaying of Mahishasura: Devi Durga’s manifestation from the brilliance of the Devas.

The Jalandhara-Vrinda Saga: The power of chastity and the birth of the Tulsi plant.

The Narasimha Manifestation: The pillar, the twilight, and the protection of Prahlada.

The Vamana Steps: Humbling King Bali and measuring the three worlds.

The Ganesha-Kartikeya Race: The definition of "The World" (Parents vs. Universe).

The Sati Atma-Daha: The sacrifice at Daksha’s Yagya.

The Markandeya Victory: Conquering death (Mrityunjaya) through Shiva’s grace.

The Syamantaka Mani: Krishna’s search for the cursed jewel.

The Liberation of Gajendra: The cry for help and Vishnu’s swift arrival.

The Tale of King Harishchandra: The ultimate test of Truth (Satya).

The Birth of Kartikeya: The convergence of five elements to destroy Taraka.

The Tripurantaka: Shiva destroying the three floating cities of the Asuras.

The Descent of the Kavacha: Indra’s deception to take Karna’s earrings.

The Curse of Bhrigu: Why Lord Vishnu takes human incarnations.

The Mohini Avatar: The trickery during the distribution of Amrita.

The Tale of Sudama: The value of a handful of flattened rice.

The Prithu Maharaja: The milking of the Earth (Bhumi).

The Kapila-Gita: Lord Kapila teaching Sankhya philosophy to Devahuti.

The Ribhu Gita: The non-dual teachings between Sage Ribhu and Nidagha.

The Slaying of Madhu-Kaitabha: The first battle at the dawn of creation.

The Story of Upamanyu: The boy who received the ocean of milk.
